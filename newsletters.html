November 2013
With Thanksgiving coming up, I think about the importance of being with family. therefore, I selected this picture of me and my future wife, kids, dogs and house. Family means the world to me. I look forward to one day starting a family and raising children.

We are heading off to Nicaragua for Thanksgiving where I will teach art to kids at a local school and we will donate art supplies to the school. Our Thanksgiving will be very special and I hope yours will be also!

Happy Thanksgiving!

My Best Day Ever at Cedar Point!

Message from Ethan

The Google picture is the picture that has six out of the sixteen roller coasters at Cedar Point, in Sandusky, Ohio. Left to right: GateKeeper, Corkscrew, Raptor, Mantis, Wicked Twister, and Maverick. I rode them all with my friends. It was awesome! Ethan donates a portion of all proceeds to children's charities in Los Angeles, and recently donated art to raise money for 18 organizations devoted to helping children.
